Park Rules anD Regulations

  • Know your limits and abilities, you are responsible of your own safety.
  • The skate park is to be used on a first come, first served basis. Do not use the skate park if it is too crowded for you to skate freely
  • Skaters SHOULD wear helmets, knee pads, elbox pads and wrist guards at all times.
  • Do not use the park when the skating surface is wet or other hazardous conditions exist. Report such conditions immediately to Skatepark's Administrator's Office.
  • A responsible adult must accompany skaters under the age of 12; supervision is encouraged for all skaters under the age of 18.
  • Amplified sound, organized events or sales of any kind are ONLY allowed with written permission from Skatepark Administrator's Office.
  • Littering is probihited. Permittee is responsible for cleaning and restoring to its proper condition all areas of the Park affected by the permittee's use.
  • Food and drink are not permitted in the skating area.
  • Report any illegal activity including vandalism and graffiti immediately to Skatepark Administrator's Office.
  • DO NOT leave important things in the Skatepark. The City Government of Antipolo is not responsible or liable for any personal property loss or damage
  • The City Government of Antipolo reserves the right to close the skate park for events, emergencies, or when conditions necessitate.
  • Violation of any may result in your removal from skate park and/or other sanctions including revoking of rights to use the skate park.
  • Skaters are probihited to bring the following:
    • 1. Drugs and all other forms of contraband;
    • 2. Firearms and/or deadly or bladed weapons and objects;
    • 3. Animals and/or pets.
    • 4. Alcoholic beverages; and,
    • 5. Flammable substaances, hazardous chemicals, and the like.
  • Skaters are probihited from:
    • 1. Smoking and vaping;
    • 2. Gambling;
    • 3. Spitting on the skate park;
    • 4. Vandalizing or destroying any and/or part of the facility's furniture, fixtures, appurtenances, and other related accessories.

Reminders:

Wear helmets at all times inside the skating area. NO Helmet NO Ride.

Youth ages 18 years old and below must wear elbow and knee pads.
