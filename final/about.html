About

Located in Sitio Cabading, Antipolo City, this skate park is one of the largest in the country and notably the first indoor facility in both CALABARZON and the National Capital Region. The project, a collaborative effort between Mayor Jun Ynares and Senator Pia Cayetano, kicked off in 2024 with the aim of providing local youth a safe alternative to skating on busy streets. With safety and convenience as top priorities, the facility features dedicated skating areas for skateboarding and BMX riding, as well as gender-inclusive and PWD-accessible amenities. After months of dedicated construction, the park officially opened on Saturday, May 10, 2025, marking a major milestone in Antipolo Cityâ€™s commitment to promoting extreme sports in a world-class setting.
